Papyra

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

This is a template privacy policy for a self-hosted Papyra deployment. The organisation or individual operating this instance (“the operator”) is the data controller and is responsible for reviewing, customising, and keeping this policy accurate for their jurisdiction before relying on it.

1. Overview

Papyra is a self-hosted PDF toolkit. This policy explains what information is processed when you use this instance and how it is handled. Because this is a self-hosted deployment, your data is processed on infrastructure controlled by the operator, not by any third-party vendor.

2. Information we process

  • Account information — if login is enabled, your username, and optionally your email address and team membership, are stored so you can sign in and be assigned an access plan.
  • Documents you upload — files are processed to perform the operation you request (for example merge, split, compress or convert). They are handled in memory or in temporary storage for the duration of processing.
  • Technical and usage data — standard server logs (such as request times and error information) may be recorded by the operator for security and reliability. Optional, opt-in usage analytics are only collected if the operator explicitly enables them.

3. How your documents are handled

PDF operations run on this server. The application is designed to be stateless with respect to your files: uploaded documents and their processed results are not retained beyond what is required to complete your request and return the result to you. The content of your documents is not tracked, sold, or shared with third parties.

4. Cookies

Essential cookies are used to keep you signed in and to remember basic preferences; these are required for the application to function. Any non-essential analytics cookies are used only if the operator has enabled analytics and you have consented.

5. Data retention

Uploaded files are removed once processing is complete. Account information is retained for as long as your account exists on this instance; contact the operator to have your account and associated data removed.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Because this is a self-hosted instance, these requests are handled by the operator of this deployment — please contact your administrator.

7. Changes to this policy

The operator may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions or requests relating to this instance, contact the administrator who operates this Papyra deployment.
